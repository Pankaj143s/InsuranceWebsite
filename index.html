LIC's Jeevan Kiran

A comprehensive term insurance plan with a return of premiums on maturity, providing financial protection during the policy term.

Features:

  • • Comprehensive Term Insurance Plan
  • • Death Benefit: Sum Assured + 105% of premiums (Regular) or 125% (Single Premium)
  • • Maturity Benefit: Return of all premiums paid
  • • Surrender: After 2 years (Regular Premium), anytime (Single Premium)
  • • Loan: Available after 2 years
  • • Sum Assured: Min ₹15,00,000

LIC's Jeevan Utsav

A whole life insurance plan offering regular income and financial security with guaranteed additions and annual survival benefits.

Features:

  • • Non-Linked, Whole Life Insurance Plan
  • • Death Benefit: Sum Assured + Guaranteed Additions
  • • Survival Benefit: 10% of Sum Assured annually
  • • Surrender: After 2 years
  • • Loan: Available after 2 years
  • • Sum Assured: Min ₹5,00,000

LIC's Amritbaal

A savings life insurance plan designed to meet the higher educational and other needs of children, offering guaranteed returns.

Features:

  • • Non-Linked, Savings Plan for Children
  • • Death Benefit: Sum Assured + Guaranteed Addition
  • • Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured + Guaranteed Addition
  • • Surrender: After 2 years (Regular), anytime (Single)
  • • Loan: Available after 2 years (Regular), 3 months (Single)
  • • Sum Assured: Min ₹2,00,000

LIC's New Jeevan Anand

Features:

  • • Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured + Bonus + Final Bonus

  • • Loan: Available after 2 years
